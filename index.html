We've been Making The Delicious Foods Since 1999

To get the real taste of authentic South Indian, Chinese cuisine one need to visit Eat99 a trusted name in South Indian dishes at the heart of the city of joy.

Eat99 being incorporated on 6th of October 1999, is serving the food loving people of Kolkata with a variety of South Indian and Chinese dishes starting from a range of Dosa’s, Hakka Noodles’s, Biryani’s, Pizza’s, etc. to choose from at their Biswa Bangla Sarani, Dash Drone, Action Area IID, Newtown,Kolkata 700157.
